We are part of VPS Healthcare, one of the largest healthcare groups in the Middle East, with 20 operational hospitals, more than 125 medical centres, 10,000 employees, and medical support services across the Middle East, Europe and India.

Emergency medical and ambulance services The go-to provider for emergency and ambulance medical services in the region

Our experienced and highly-trained medical professionals have the back-up of one of the largest private fleets of ambulances in Abu Dhabi.

All our vehicles are available from strategically-located ambulance stations on a 24-hour basis. They are equipped for acute emergencies, from initial evaluation to resuscitation, treatment, investigation, stabilisation and monitoring. In addition, each ambulance carries a range of emergency drugs that are customised to clients’ needs.

Occupational health services Targeting the health concerns of the working population

Our long-standing relationship with a large number of companies throughout the UAE is notable for two reasons.

Firstly, our partnership approach to healthcare provision ensures that the Occupational Health (OH) Services we provide are absolutely industry-specific. Secondly, any related medical assessments are job or sector-specific to ensure that all the relevant aspects of an employee’s health at work can be fully covered.

These include, for example, pre-placement medical screening for new recruits, in-service medical check-ups for existing staff and exit medical screening for outgoing staff.

One of our principal clients
Al Jaber Group

Mobile clinics Healthcare for everyone, regardless of location

Mobile healthcare, particularly in remote or rural areas, has assumed vital importance as a link to the outside world.

We use specially-equipped trucks that feature secure examination rooms, X-ray and ECG facilities, and laboratory services. This means people who would not normally be able to access quality healthcare by reason of distance, cost or disability can now do so with RPM’s Mobile Project. It brings medical services direct to their doorstep – wherever they are.
